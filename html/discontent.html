discontent

___

Noun

/dɪskənˈtɛnt/

__

1 dissatisfaction with one's circumstances; lack of contentment

    the discontents of the working class
    voters voiced discontent with both parties

1.1 a person who is dissatisfied, typically with the prevailing social or political situation

    the cause attracted a motley crew of discontents and zealots

/dɪskənˈtɛnt/
