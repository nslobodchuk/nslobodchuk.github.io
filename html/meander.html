meander

___

Verb

/mɪˈandə/

__

1 (of a river or road) follow a winding course

    a river that meandered gently through a meadow

1.1 wander at random

    kids meandered in and out

1.2 (of language, thought, etc.) proceed aimlessly or with little purpose

    a stylish offbeat thriller which occasionally meanders

___

Noun

/mɪˈandə/

__

1 a winding curve or bend of a river or road

    the river flows in sweeping meanders

1.1 an indirect or aimless journey

    a leisurely meander round the twisting coastline road

1.2 an ornamental pattern of winding or interlocking lines.
