backstop

___

Noun

/ˈbakstɒp/

__

1 a thing placed at the rear of something as a barrier or support

    pull back harder until you reach the backstop on the stick

1.1 a high fence or similar structure behind the home plate area.

1.2 a catcher.

1.3 an emergency precaution or last resort

    the human operator has to act as the ultimate backstop when things go badly wrong

___

Verb

/ˈbakstɒp/

__

1 act as backstop for.

1.1 act as goaltender for

    the man who backstopped the Edmonton Oilers

1.2 support or reinforce

    the founding banks were backstopping the loans
