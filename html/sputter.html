sputter

___

Verb

/ˈspʌtə/

__

1 make a series of soft explosive or spitting sounds

    the engine sputtered and stopped

1.1 speak in a series of incoherent bursts as a result of strong emotion

    ‘But … but …’ she sputtered

1.2 emit with a spitting sound

    the goose is in the oven, sputtering fat

1.3 proceed in a spasmodic and feeble way

    strikes in the public services sputtered on

2 deposit (metal) on a surface by using fast ions to eject particles of it from a target.

2.1 coat (a surface) with a spray of metal particles emitted from a target that is bombarded with fast ions.
